On Deck

Support

On Deck is currently being prepared for wider beta use. If you are testing through TestFlight, the fastest way to report issues is through TestFlight’s built-in feedback flow so screenshots and device details are attached automatically.

Best way to report a bug

  • Tell us which device you are using.
  • Describe what you tapped and what happened instead of the expected result.
  • Include whether the issue happened on phone, web, or both.

Common beta issues to mention clearly

  • Sync delay or missing joke updates between devices.
  • Problems pairing the phone workspace to the hosted desktop web app.
  • Microphone permission or recording playback problems.
  • Open mic listing, map, or calendar issues.
  • Unexpected workspace disconnects or missing paired browser access.
