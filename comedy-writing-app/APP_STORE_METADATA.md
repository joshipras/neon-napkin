## Description

On Deck is a workspace for comedians to write, organize, rehearse, and track material.

Use On Deck to:

- Capture joke ideas quickly
- Organize material across set stages
- Open and edit jokes from phone or web
- Record rehearsals and keep takes attached to each joke
- Add tags and move bits through your workflow
- Track your writing streak
- Browse open mics and add them to your calendar
- Sync your workspace between phone and desktop web with a temporary pairing code

Whether you are brainstorming a new premise, tightening a set, or planning what mics to hit tonight, On Deck keeps your comedy workflow in one place.
